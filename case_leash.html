Case Study: Gap-Fill Strategy (Dog Leash)

Using the "Attack & Defense" model to capture missing high-value traffic while protecting the brand.

The Strategy

Identified that the competitor was ignoring "Heavy Duty" and "Reflective" keywords. Built a specialized "Attack" ad group.

Key Pivot

Shifted budget from "Pet Leash" (expensive/generic) to "Anti-Pull Dog Leash" (problem-solving/high-intent).

Audit Findings

  • Title Gap: Missing 150+ characters of SEO potential.
  • Visuals: Lifestyle shot used as Main Image (TOS Violation).
