Case Study: ROI Recovery (Silicon Spatula)

Focused on rescuing a high-spend, low-efficiency campaign by moving away from bleeding broad match terms.

The Problem

Campaign was spending heavily on "Kitchen Tools" and "Small Gifts" with zero conversions. ACoS was unsustainable at 48%.

The Solution

Implemented Keyword Graduation. Moved profitable search terms from Auto to Manual Exact with optimized bids.

Audit Findings

  • Main Image: Amateur lighting, failing to stand out against competitors.
  • Title: Short and missing volume-drivers like "Heat Resistant" and "BPA Free".
