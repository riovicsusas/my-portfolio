PPC Expert Insights

What is your process for reducing high ACoS? +
My recovery process starts with Negative Keyword Mining to cut immediate waste, followed by Day-Parting analysis to ensure spend happens during "Power Hours," and finally, a Bid-to-Value adjustment based on historical conversion rates.
How do you handle a new product launch? +
I use a 3-step launch funnel: 1. Automated Discovery for search term harvesting. 2. Market-Share Attack using competitor ASIN targeting (PAT). 3. Aggressive Exact Match scale on high-relevance seed keywords discovered in step 1.
Why use Manual campaigns over Automatic? +
Automatic campaigns are for discovery; Manual campaigns are for profit. Manual gives us control over bids, placements, and exact search term matching, which is the only way to achieve a stable, low ACoS long-term.
