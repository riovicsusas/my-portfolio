Professional Workflow

1. Research

The Foundation

  • Market Analysis Competitor pricing & keyword gaps.
  • Listing Audit SEO readiness before ad spend.
  • Targeting Setup Identifying seed keywords & ASINs.

2. Structure

The Funnel Approach

  • Discovery Layer Auto Campaigns to harvest high-performing search terms.
  • Validation Layer Move harvested terms to Manual Broad/Phrase.
  • Profit Layer Graduate best keywords to Exact Match.
  • Defense & Conquest PAT attributes targeting relative ASINs.

3. Optimize

Weekly Maintenance Cycle

  • Negative Mining Exact match negatives for bleeding terms.
  • Bid Optimization Math-based adjustments vs Target ACoS.
  • Placement Adjustments Modifiers for Top-of-Search performance.

4. Scale

Strategic Expansion

  • Keyword Graduation Scaling terms with proven CVR.
  • Budget Day-Parting Allocating for high-traffic "Power Hours".
  • Brand Growth Sponsored Brands (Video/Headline) focus.
